Tracks connections between characters for richer multi-character content.

## Rivalries
- Batman vs Joker (DC) -- archetypal hero/villain dynamic
- Thor vs Loki (Marvel) -- sibling rivalry with cosmic stakes
- Captain America vs Red Skull (Marvel) -- ideological opposition
- Spider-Man vs Green Goblin (Marvel) -- personal vendetta
