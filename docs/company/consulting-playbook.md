# Consulting Playbook

Zero owns the operating loop for ADA AI LLC consulting. The goal is to
turn Adam's AI adoption expertise, software-building ability, second-brain
system, and practical automation stack into a focused consulting practice that
can later feed the software-product studio.

## Positioning

ADA AI LLC helps small companies and founder-led teams adopt AI safely:
workflow discovery, agent design, data/context readiness, automation buildout,
guardrails, and internal dashboards.

The first public surfaces are `adappliedai.com` for ADA AI LLC and
`adamdoherty.com` for Adam's founder presence. Public copy, client emails,
proposals, SOWs, legal disclaimers, and pricing changes are high-risk company
actions and require approval before sending or publishing.

## Initial offers

| Offer | Buyer | Outcome | Evidence needed |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI Adoption Readiness Audit | SMB owner or operator | Ranked workflow map, risk register, first 30-day roadmap | Discovery notes, workflow screenshots, final report |
| Agent Workflow Sprint | Founder or ops lead | One internal agent workflow with approval gates | SOW, implementation checklist, test log |
| Company OS Setup | Solo founder or small team | Task, docs, dashboards, and second-brain operating system | Repo/docs handoff, task board, weekly review convention |
| AI Governance Starter Kit | Regulated or finance-adjacent team | AI use policy, vendor register, approval matrix | Attorney-reviewed disclaimer queue |

## Sales workflow

1. Capture lead in Zero CRM.
2. Attach source, relationship, pain signal, and likely offer.
3. Draft discovery questions.
4. Run discovery call and store notes.
5. Create opportunity with stage, next step, value, and deadline.
6. Draft proposal/SOW.
7. Route proposal through approval if any terms, pricing, claims, or client
   commitments are included.
8. On acceptance, create client project, delivery checklist, document folder,
   and weekly status cadence.

## Delivery checklist

- Intake form and business-context summary.
- Current workflow map.
- AI readiness score.
- Data and tools inventory.
- Automation candidates ranked by value, risk, and feasibility.
- Approval and security policy.
- Implementation plan.
- Final report or dashboard.
- Follow-up tasks and support offer.

## Zero responsibilities

Zero may draft discovery questions, summarize calls, create CRM records, update
internal tasks, prepare weekly reports, and recommend next actions.

Zero must require approval before sending client communications, publishing
marketing claims, changing pricing, signing terms, or making legal/tax claims.

## Metrics

- Qualified leads.
- Discovery calls booked.
- Proposals sent.
- Proposal acceptance rate.
- Revenue booked.
- Delivery cycle time.
- Client follow-up tasks completed.
- Case studies approved for publication.
