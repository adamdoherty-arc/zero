# Product And Robotics Roadmap

## Consulting First

The consulting offer validates the market before the software product is pushed
hard. Start with AI adoption readiness audits, workflow automation sprints, and
agent-operating-system implementation for founder-led and small business teams.

## Product Thesis Candidates

| Product | Initial thesis | First proof |
|---|---|---|
| AI adoption command center | Small companies need a safer way to see AI agents, tasks, approvals, vendors, and risks | Use this Company OS internally |
| Financial decision-support workspace | Personal market research and portfolio context can be useful without autonomous advice claims | Keep claims and actions human-approved |
| Second-brain operations sync | Operators need repo, Legion, and Obsidian state reconciled | Weekly review mirror and decision log |

## Robotics / 3D Printing

The robotics lab starts as internal capability:

- workstation organization and parts;
- printer calibration and maintenance;
- prototypes for internal workflows;
- client demos only after safety review.

Physical products stay under the same LLC initially. Revisit insurance, product
liability, separate entities, and terms before selling printed goods at scale.

## Phase Plan

| Phase | Goal | Exit criteria |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Operating foundation | LLC setup path, dashboard, task board, source docs |
| 1 | Consulting offer | ICP, offer, discovery, proposal, CRM, first conversations |
| 2 | Product MVP | One product thesis, MVP spec, pricing assumptions, waitlist |
| 3 | Delivery system | Client onboarding, project templates, measurement loop |
| 4 | Robotics lab | Inventory, maintenance, safety reviews, prototype queue |
| 5 | Commercial expansion | Insurance, attorney review, payment/tax automation |
