# LLC And Compliance Plan

This document is an internal operating checklist, not legal, tax, accounting, or
investment advice. Final tax positions should be reviewed by a CPA. SaaS,
financial-software, privacy, contract, and product-liability language should be
reviewed by a Florida business attorney.

## Recommended 2026 Choice

**ADA AI LLC** has been created. Treat it as the active Florida single-member
LLC baseline unless CPA or attorney review changes the tax or legal posture.

Do not elect S-Corp status in year 1. Revisit only after durable business profit
makes payroll and reasonable compensation worthwhile.

Do not place options trading in the operating company. Keep trading personal in
2026 unless a trader-tax CPA recommends a separate trading entity and election
timeline.

## Finance Setup Gate

The canonical setup packet is `finance-setup-kit.md`. The order is:

1. Confirm no EIN already exists.
2. Apply directly through the free IRS EIN assistant if no EIN exists.
3. Save the EIN confirmation letter in secure company records.
4. Sign the operating agreement and owner/banking authorization.
5. Open the ADA AI LLC business checking account using EIN, Florida formation
   docs, operating agreement, ownership statement, ID, and any local bank
   requirement.
6. Deposit the first funds as an owner contribution.
7. Document business credit-card reporting policy before any application.
8. Start books, receipt intake, home-office worksheet, asset register, robot
   record, and software/IP assignment schedule.

No equipment, robot, or software transfer should be booked at an unsupported
markup. Existing personal assets enter ADA only at documented fair market value
and CPA-approved treatment.

## Entity Revisit Triggers

Revisit the structure with a CPA or attorney when any of these become true:

- net business profit is sustainably above the practical S-Corp threshold;
- outside investors or priced equity become plausible;
- robotics or physical products create meaningful product-liability exposure;
- separate brands generate distinct revenue and liability;
- public financial-software positioning approaches regulated-advice territory;
- trading activity becomes frequent enough to evaluate trader tax status.
